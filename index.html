Doctoral research project

PaceVer

PaceVer studies multimodal emotion-cause reasoning in conversation through one linked agenda: strong structured extraction, bridgeable explanation interfaces, and verification-aware reasoning on top of fixed pair scores.

This public site is no longer one oversized landing page. It now acts as a portal into the three public-facing pillars of the project: PaceExt, BridgeProt, and the Survey that frames the broader field.

Project Logic

Why the project is organized this way

Efficiency

Keep extraction bounded

Structured MECPE should remain computationally disciplined instead of collapsing into unconstrained generation.

Completeness

Recover hard causes

Low-confidence but real causes need a second-stage path rather than being discarded by one-shot thresholding alone.

Auditability

Keep explanations checkable

Explanation quality is not only about fluency. Outputs should stay connected to explicit evidence and pair-level evaluation.
